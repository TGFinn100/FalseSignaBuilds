# False Signal

**The signal's clear. The voice is familiar. Neither should be trusted.**

Your crew is stranded on a world nobody surveyed properly, chasing readings that don't add up while the air runs thin and radiation tears static through every transmission. Radio contact is the only thing keeping you together — until you can't tell anymore whether it's static, or something answering you back.

## Features

- **Multiplayer Survival** — Land with your crew, coordinate over comms, and try to make it back off-world in one piece.
- **A Procedurally Generated World** — No two descents onto the planet look the same; chase readings across shifting terrain that never quite feels safe.
- **You're Not Alone on the Channel** — Something else on this planet has access to your comms — and it's a better listener than you'd like.
- **Full Spherical Gravity Traversal** — Walk, run, and fight around the actual curve of the planet — there's no horizon here you can trust to stay flat.
- **Independent Survival Systems** — Stamina, oxygen, and radiation exposure all drain on their own terms; the planet is trying to kill you before anything else gets the chance.
- **A Black Hole on the Horizon** — An orbiting black hole warps the sky above, a constant reminder of exactly how far from help you are.

## Current Status

False Signal is in active development. Builds shared here are for playtesting and feedback, not a finished release.

What's playable right now: Steam-based multiplayer lobbies, a procedurally generated planet you can explore on foot, comms/voice chat the Mimic can exploit, and independent survival systems (stamina, oxygen, radiation) that put real pressure on how long you can stay out there.

What to expect: this is an early build. Art, UI, and audio are placeholder-quality in places, some systems are still being tuned, and stability isn't guaranteed build-to-build. If something breaks or feels off, that feedback is genuinely useful at this stage.

## Controls & How to Play

| Action | Key |
|---|---|
| Move | WASD / Arrow keys |
| Look | Mouse |
| Sprint | Left Shift (drains stamina) |
| Crouch | C |
| Jump | Space |
| Pause / free cursor | Esc |

Controller support is present but not yet optimized.

Voice chat is **open mic** — your microphone is live for the whole session, no push-to-talk. Worth keeping in mind.

### Hosting / Joining

1. Launch the game — you're automatically placed into your own Steam lobby.
2. To play with friends, the lobby host clicks **Invite** and sends invites through Steam.
3. Invited friends accept via the Steam overlay to join the host's lobby.
4. Once everyone's in, the host clicks **Play** to start the session — everyone else connects automatically.
